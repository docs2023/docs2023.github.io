Tianjin

Located in northeastern China, is a bustling metropolis with a rich history and vibrant culture. As one of the four municipalities directly governed by the Chinese central government, Tianjin is a major economic and industrial hub.
Known for its unique blend of traditional Chinese and Western influences, Tianjin offers a fascinating mix of architectural styles, from ancient temples and traditional courtyard houses to grand colonial-era buildings. The city's iconic attractions include the Tianjin Eye, a giant Ferris wheel offering panoramic views of the city, and the beautifully preserved Italian-style concession area known as the Five Great Avenues.
Tianjin is renowned for its culinary delights, particularly its local snacks and street food. From steamed buns and savory pancakes to spicy noodles and fresh seafood, food lovers will find a wide array of delicious options to satisfy their taste buds.
In recent years, Tianjin has undergone rapid development, with modern skyscrapers, shopping malls, and entertainment complexes dotting the skyline. The city also hosts various international events and festivals, attracting visitors from around the world.
Additionally, Tianjin serves as an important transportation hub, with a major port on the Bohai Sea and well-connected railway and air networks. Its strategic location makes it a gateway to Beijing, the capital of China, as well as other parts of the country.
With its historical charm, vibrant cultural scene, and dynamic economic growth, Tianjin offers a unique blend of old and new, making it an exciting destination for both tourists and business travelers alike.


Sightseeing

Haihe River Cruise

Location:Near Dazhigu Middle Road, Hedong District, Tianjin, China

   

The Haihe River is one of the seven major rivers in China, commonly known as the mother river of Tianjin, and one of the must-see attractions in Tianjin. As a flowing scenery on the Haihe River, the Haihe River Cruise allows tourists to enjoy Tianjin's unique history, culture and beautiful natural scenery on the Haihe River.

The tour route of Tianjin Haihe River Cruise is divided into upstream and downstream. The upstream is mainly to watch the historical buildings and cultural attractions on the left bank, while the downstream is mainly to watch the natural scenery and modern buildings on the right bank. At each stop, visitors can choose to visit different attractions.

Tianjin Italian Style Street

Location: Intersection of Shengli Road and Ziyou Road, Hebei District, Tianjin, China

 

Tianjin Italian Style Street is located in Hebei District, Tianjin, the former Italian Concession, and there are nearly 200 well-preserved European buildings.

As a block with strong European style, Italian Style Street is a good place for tourists to experience exotic atmosphere. Here, tourists can taste authentic Italian food and appreciate the unique architectural style and cultural atmosphere.

The Museum covers an area of 37000 square meters and has a building areaWith the purpose of embodying strong Italian style, Tianjin Italian Style Street has built the style area into a comprehensive multi-functional area integrating tourism, commerce, leisure, entertainment and cultural museums.

The Five Avenues

Location: No. 83, Chongqing Road, Heping District, Tianjin, China.

 

Five Avenues Cultural Tourist Area refers to a rectangular area south of Chengdu Road, north of Machang Road, east of Xikang Road, and west of the intersection of Machang Road and Nanjing Road. Call it the "Five Avenues".

There are more than 2,000 garden-style houses built in the 1920s and 1930s with different national architectural styles in the Wudao Avenue area, with a construction area of more than 1 million square meters. Among the more than 300 most typical buildings, there are 89 British-style buildings, 41 Italian-style buildings, 6 French-style buildings, 4 German-style buildings, 3 Spanish-style buildings, and many Renaissance-style buildings, Classical architecture, eclectic architecture, Baroque architecture, courtyard architecture, and Chinese and Western architecture, etc., are known as the "International Architecture Expo".

Eye of Tianjin

Location:On Yongle Bridge, Sancha River Estuary, Hebei District, Tianjin, China

   

The Tientsin Eye Ferris wheel, also known as The Yongle Bridge Tientsin Eye, is located on the Yongle Bridge at the intersection of Ligongci Street and Wuma Road, Hebei District, Tianjin, across the sea The river connects Hebei District and Hongqiao District. It is a ferris wheel built across the river and combined with a bridge and a wheel. It has both sightseeing and transportation functions. It is one of the landmarks of Tianjin.

It takes 28 minutes for the Ferris wheel to rotate a circle. When it reaches the highest point, the surrounding scenery can be seen at a glance, and even the scenery within a radius of 40 kilometers can be seen. The diameter is 110 meters. The cockpit can accommodate 8 people and can accommodate 384 people for sightseeing at the same time. The turning speed is slow and not exciting, but it is interesting to see the vehicles passing by on both sides.

Tianjin ancient cultural street

Location: Tongbei Road, Nankai District, Tianjin, China

   

Ancient Culture Street is located in Nankai District, Tianjin, with Tianhou Temple as the center. It is a concentration of famous time-honored brands and handicraft shops in Tianjin, and it is one of the must-see places in Tianjin. There are many time-honored shops in Tianjin with strong folk characteristics, such as Qiaoxiangge selling cloisonne, Suzhou embroidery and lacquerware. While shopping, you can also enjoy Tianjin's special delicacies, such as Goubuli buns, pancakes, Tianjin cannabis flowers, etc., which will definitely satisfy the appetite of foodies. Every year from before the Spring Festival to the Lantern Festival, the Ancient Culture Street is the busiest, and there are also various activities and temple fairs.

St. Joseph Cathedral

Location:No. 9 Xining Road, Heping District, Tianjin, China

   

The largest Catholic church in Tianjin is also the cathedral of the Catholic Tianjin Diocese, a typical Baroque style building. The overall building is in the shape of "pin", the balcony is made of yellow and safflower bricks, the emerald green round belly-shaped spire is built on the top, and the semicircular arched windows are under the eaves. The church holds religious activities every morning, and on Sundays and Catholic holidays, there are religious activities in the morning and evening. Walking out of the Xikai Church is the International Style Street, scattered with large and small coffee shops, western restaurants, full of flavor.

Jinwan Plaza

Location:No. 48, Jiefang North Road, Heping District, Tianjin, China

     

Jinwan Plaza is located beside the Hai River, opposite to Tianjin Railway Station across the Hai River, and is one of the most prosperous areas in downtown Tianjin. The biggest highlight is its cultural heritage. The diverse composition of Tianjin's modern architectural styles highlights the characteristics of the era when Chinese and Western cultures meet. In addition, it is also the best place to enjoy the day and night scenery of the Haihe River. The high-rise building behind it is a bustling modern city, which is gorgeous and charming when it is lit at night. The European buildings around the square and on both sides of the river also constitute the whole landscape, which is one of the iconic viewing spots in Tianjin.

Fantawild Adventure

Location:No. 4888, Zhongsheng Avenue, Sino-Singapore Eco-city, Binhai New Area, Tianjin, China

   

Located in the ecological island of Sino-Singapore Eco-city in Binhai New Area, Tianjin, it is a unique fourth-generation high-tech theme park in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region, suitable for all ages. It consists of 27 themed project areas, including Flying Over Limits, Magic Castle, Bear Adventure, Blue Earth, Oriental Charm, Conch Bay, Tanggula Snow Mountain, and Jinmen Grand Canyon. With science fiction and interactive experience as the biggest feature, most of the projects are at the top level of global theme park projects, and have the reputation of "Oriental Fantasy Paradise" and "Asian Science Fiction Magic".

Tianjin Museum

Location:Tianjin Cultural Center, Pingjiang Road, Hexi District, Tianjin, China

     

The predecessor of Tianjin Museum can be traced back to the Tianjin Museum established in 1918. Although it was newly built in recent years and has lost a lot of ancient flavor, it is still the main place to inherit the local history and culture of Tianjin. The museum has 4 floors. The first floor is "The Origin of Tianjin Humanities", the second floor is the exhibition of fine cultural relics collected in the museum, the third floor is "Tianjin in the past 100 years of China", and the fourth floor is the exhibition of calligraphy and painting. Learn about the historical changes and local culture of the entire Tianjin here, and the treasures of the town hall include the axis of Fan Kuan's "Snow Scenery and Cold Forest" in the Northern Song Dynasty, the Qing Qianlong enamel colorful peony chicken pattern jade pot spring bottle, the Western Zhou Taibao Ding, and the topaz pig dragon. Waiting will make the trip worth it. From time to time, various themed exhibitions will also be held.

Young Marshal House

Location:No. 78, Chifeng Road, Heping District, Tianjin, China

     

Zhang Xueliang's Former Residence Museum is also known as the Marshal's Mansion, where the Zhang family lived in the 1920s and 1930s. The building is luxurious, beautiful and generous, mainly in Western Baroque style. The rooms are well furnished, and the stairs, floors, doors and windows are all made of Philippine wood. After more than two years of renovation, the former residence of Zhang Xueliang has reappeared in the present and the old. The music, decoration, clothing, furniture, and characters here reproduce the original life of the Zhang family in the French Concession in Tianjin.

Food Culture

       

Goubuli Bao is a famous traditional snack in Tianjin, China. The snack is made of flour, pork and other materials. It was first created in 1858 (Xianfeng period of the Qing Dynasty) and has a history of more than 100 years. Snacks are the first of the "Three Wonders of Tianjin" and one of the time-honored brands in China. Goubuli steamed stuffed buns have carefully selected noodles and fillings, strict manufacturing techniques, and beautiful appearance, especially the buns with well-proportioned pleats, each with no less than 15 pleats. Steamed buns just out of the cage, fresh but not greasy, fragrant and delicious

Rice cracker, generally called Gaba dish by Tianjin people, is a unique roadside snack. Spread mung bean noodles on a pancake like pancakes, then cut into finger-wide strips, and pour in the pre-made marinade. Milk, chive flower juice, garlic juice and sesame sauce, with sesame seed cakes and fruits.

Baosanyang, also known as Laobaosan, is one of the famous traditional dishes in Tianjin. The main ingredients are pork, pork liver and pork kidney, etc. The mouth is a bit heavy and salty. Pork kidney has the effects of invigorating kidney qi, clearing the bladder, eliminating stagnation, and relieving thirst. It can be used to treat kidney deficiency, low back pain, edema, deafness and other diseases. Those with high blood lipids and high cholesterol should not eat it.

There is no doubt that pancake fruit is the representative of early Tianjin people. Spread a spoonful of mung bean noodles on the pan, crack an egg, quickly spread and turn over, put the scorpion or fruit grate, spread the sauce, chili sauce, and sprinkle chopped green onion, the thick noodle fragrance and the aroma of eggs are early It has already tempted the diners to be hungry. The small pancake fruit shows Tianjin people's attention to food and breakfast culture.

Ripe pear cake is also called bowl cake, which has nothing to do with pears. It is a very traditional folk snack in Tianjin. It also has a serious scientific name "Zenger Cake". "Zeng" (sounds the same as "gift") is an ancient cooking utensil with many small holes at the bottom through which steam can steam the food in the container. When making it, first put the ground rice noodles in a wooden steamer, steam the rice noodles with the steam in a steamer, and then put various jams on top.
